How Combo+ works

Create your account

If you already have Disney+ or Star+, you can use your existing email and password to access both services. If you do not have any service, you must create an account to start the subscription process.

Download Disney+ and Star+

Use your favorite devices or go directly to disneyplus.com and starplus.com to see what you like, whenever and wherever you want.

Existing subscriber? No problem

If you already have a monthly subscription for Disney+ or Star+, the amount you pay will be deducted from the final price of Combo+. If you have an annual subscription, we will divide the price you paid into 12 equal parts and deduct that price on a monthly basis from the final price of Combo+.
